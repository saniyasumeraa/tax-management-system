Online Tax Calculator

The Income Tax Department provides various online tools and services to help taxpayers manage their taxes easily. These services include tax calculation, return filing, and status tracking.

Steps to Use e-Services

  1. Visit the official Income Tax e-Filing portal
  2. Login using PAN and password
  3. Select the required service (e-Filing, Refund, etc.)
  4. Fill in the required details
  5. Submit and verify

  • Advance Tax Payment

  • AIS (Annual Information Statement)
