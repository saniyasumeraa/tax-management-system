How to Fill Income Tax Details

Basic Details

Select financial year and age group. Tax slabs differ based on age and regime.

Income Details

Enter total income from salary, interest, rent, and other sources.

Deductions

Enter deductions under sections like 80C, 80D, and 80TTA to reduce taxable income.

Allowances

Enter allowances like HRA, transport, and medical benefits.

Guide for Filling Details

1. Basic Details

  • Select the correct Financial Year (example: FY 2025-26)
  • Select your Age Group (important for tax calculation)
  • Tax slab rates are shown for reference

2. Income Details

  • Salary: Total annual salary received
  • Interest Income: Bank FD, savings interest
  • Rental Income: Income from house rent
  • Other Income: Any additional income (freelancing, etc.)

3. Deductions

  • 80C: Investments like LIC, PPF, ELSS (max ₹1.5 lakh)
  • 80D: Medical insurance premium
  • 80TTA: Savings account interest deduction

4. Allowances

  • HRA: House Rent Allowance received
  • Transport: Travel allowance from employer
  • Medical: Medical reimbursement/allowance
Tip: Enter correct values to get accurate tax calculation.

Income Tax – General Knowledge

1. What is Income Tax?

Income tax is a direct tax paid by individuals and businesses to the government based on the income they earn. It is one of the main sources of government revenue.

2. What is Income Tax Slab?

Income tax slab is a system where different portions of income are taxed at different rates. Higher income falls into higher slabs and is taxed at higher percentages.

3. How is Income Tax Calculated?

Tax calculation follows a simple process:

  • Total Income = Salary + Interest + Rent + Other Income
  • Total Deduction = 80C + 80D + 80TTA + Allowances
  • Taxable Income = Total Income − Total Deductions
  • Tax is applied based on slab rates
  • 4% Health & Education Cess is added to final tax

4. Old vs New Tax Regime

  • Old Regime: Higher tax rates but allows deductions
  • New Regime: Lower tax rates but fewer deductions

5. Important Tax Sections

  • 80C: Investment deduction (₹1.5 lakh)
  • 80D: Medical insurance
  • 80TTA: Interest deduction
  • 87A: Rebate for low income

6. Why Do We Pay Income Tax?

Income tax helps the government fund infrastructure, education, healthcare, defense, and other public services.

Understanding income tax helps you plan savings and reduce tax legally.
