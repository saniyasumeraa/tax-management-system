About Income Tax

What is Income Tax?

Income tax is a direct tax imposed by the government on income earned by individuals and businesses. It is governed by the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Objectives

  • Revenue generation for government
  • Economic development
  • Reduction of inequality
  • Public welfare funding

Types of Income

  • Salary
  • House Property
  • Business/Profession
  • Capital Gains
  • Other Sources

Importance

Income tax plays a crucial role in nation building by funding infrastructure, education, healthcare, and defense.

Income Tax Calculator

An Income Tax Calculator helps individuals estimate their tax liability based on income, deductions, and applicable tax slabs. It is useful for financial planning and quick calculations.

Income Tax Slabs (India Example)

  • Up to ₹2,50,000 – No tax
  • ₹2,50,001 to ₹5,00,000 – 5%
  • ₹5,00,001 to ₹10,00,000 – 20%
  • Above ₹10,00,000 – 30%

Common Deductions

  • Section 80C (Investments)
  • Section 80D (Medical Insurance)
  • Home Loan Interest
  • Education Loan Interest

Tax Calculation Overview

Income tax is calculated based on annual income and applicable tax slabs. Deductions and exemptions are applied to reduce taxable income. Online tools help taxpayers estimate their tax easily.
